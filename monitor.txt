azure monitor: use autoscale to add/remove resources to minimize costs and ensure performance -- you can create rules based on metrics collected by a.monitor to match resources to application load

***

azure monitor helps maximize availability and performance of your apps and services

- delivers a comprehensive solution for collecting, analyzing, and acting on telemetry from your cloud and on-prem devices

- this info helps you understand how your apps are performing and proactively identify issues affecting them and the resources they depend on

things you can do with azure monitor:
- detect and diagnose issues with app insights
- correlate infra issues with VM / Container insights
- drill into monitoring data with log analytics
- support ops at scale with smart alerts and automated actions
- create visualizations with dashboards and workbooks
- collect data from monitored resources using a.monitor metrics
